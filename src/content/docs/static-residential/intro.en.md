# Static Residential Proxies · Introduction

![](/src/assets/img/Static-residential/4.png)
**Static Residential Proxies** are proxy products tailored for various business scenarios. This chapter will help you understand the product features, applicable scenarios, and basic architecture.

## Product Features

* Stable and reliable IP resource pool
* Coverage across major countries and regions worldwide
* Support for both HTTP and SOCKS5 protocols
* Username & Password authentication method

## Applicable Scenarios

* Cross-border e-commerce and social media account management
* Social media matrix marketing and account nurturing
* Advertising campaign management
* Session-based web scraping (login-required data extraction)
* Ad verification and brand asset protection
