# Static Residential Proxies · FAQ

## 1. What should I do if the connection times out?

Please check your local network, firewall settings, and whether your account has an overdue balance. You can use cURL to troubleshoot the connection first.

## 2. How do I delete expired IPs?

* Only IPs that have already expired can be selected and deleted.

## 3. Can I renew the IP and keep it the same?

Yes! The IP address will not change after renewal.

## 4. Is city or state targeting supported for purchase?

* No, it is not supported.
* We only support purchasing based on the IP's ASN (Autonomous System Number) selection.

## 5. What is the bandwidth of the static residential proxies?

* Dedicated 20 Mbps per IP.

:::tip 💡
You can verify this by visiting `fast.com`.
:::

## 6. Do static residential proxies require additional traffic/data purchases?

* No additional traffic purchase is required.
* Static residential proxies come with unlimited data traffic.

## 7. Is the UDP protocol supported?

* Yes, it is supported.
* You can enable it yourself directly within the IP management interface.
