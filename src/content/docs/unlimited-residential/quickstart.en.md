# Unlimited Residential Proxies · Quick Start

Follow these steps to set up your [Unlimited Proxies](https://www.lumiproxy.com/user/unlimited/pricing/) in **LumiProxy**. Log in, create your proxy zone, and verify your account to get started.

:::tip 💡
Unlimited residential proxies do not support targeting specific countries; they use a global rotating pool instead.
:::

## Step 1: Register an Account

[Register](https://www.lumiproxy.com/signup/)

Visit the official website to register and complete your account setup.

## Step 2: Purchase a Plan

Select a plan in the dashboard that fits your business scale and complete the payment.
