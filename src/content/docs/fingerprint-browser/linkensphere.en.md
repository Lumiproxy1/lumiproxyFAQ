# How to Integrate LumiProxy with Sphere Browser

> Integrate LumiProxy with Sphere Browser to manage multiple accounts securely and anonymously. Follow this step-by-step guide for a seamless configuration.

## How to Integrate LumiProxy with Sphere Browser

### Step 1. Download and Install Sphere Browser

1. Visit the **[Sphere Browser Official Website](https://linkensphere.info/en/#)** and download the application.
2. Install the software on your device and log in using your account credentials.
3. Open Sphere Browser and click on **Proxy** to begin configuring your settings.

![](/src/assets/img/fingerprint/linkensphere/linkensphere1.avif)

### Step 2. Configure Proxy Settings

1. In the profile creation window, provide a unique and descriptive name in the **Profile Name** field to easily identify your browser instance.
2. Head over to your [LumiProxy Dashboard](https://www.lumiproxy.com/user/dashboard/).
3. Under the **Access Details** section of the **Overview** tab, use a text editor to assemble your connection string in the following format: `host:port:username:password`.
4. Return to Sphere Browser and paste your credentials into the corresponding fields.
5. Click the **Create** button (with the checkmark icon) to save your proxy configurations.

:::info
For geo-targeted proxies, format your username as `lumi-username-area-XX` (for example, `lumi-username-area-US`) to route your session through a specific location.
:::

### Step 3. Launch and Verify

1. Locate the profile you have just configured.
2. Click **Check Proxy** to ensure that the connection is successful.

![](/src/assets/img/fingerprint/linkensphere/linkensphere2.avif)

Integrating LumiProxy with Sphere Browser ensures a secure and anonymous browsing experience tailored to your exact operational requirements. Whether managing multiple profiles or bypassing geo-restrictions, this configuration delivers the necessary privacy and flexibility.
