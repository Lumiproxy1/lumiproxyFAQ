# How to Set Up LumiProxy in GoLogin

> Enhance your web scraping and multi-account management with GoLogin and LumiProxy to achieve powerful anti-detection capabilities and flexible proxy control.

## GoLogin Proxy Integration

Follow these steps to integrate LumiProxy proxies into GoLogin:

### Install GoLogin

**Download and install GoLogin from the [GoLogin Official Website](https://gologin.com/).**

### Create an Account

Log into GoLogin to begin setting up your browser profiles.

### Create a New Profile

Click **+ Add Profile** to enter the basic information for your new browser configuration.
![](/src/assets/img/fingerprint/GoLogin/GoLogin1.avif)

### Configure Proxy Settings

Input your LumiProxy proxy connection details:

* **Proxy Type**: Select `HTTP` or `SOCKS5` (based on your proxy protocol).
* **Host**: Enter `us.lumiproxy.io`.
* **Port**: Use the port number provided in your [LumiProxy Dashboard](https://www.lumiproxy.com/user/dashboard/).
* **Username**: Enter your LumiProxy proxy `username`.
* **Password**: Enter your LumiProxy proxy `password`.

:::info ℹ️
**You can also specify a target country/region for your proxy session. For example, entering `lumi-username-area-US` will route your connection through a United States exit node.**
:::

![](/src/assets/img/fingerprint/GoLogin/GoLogin2.avif)

### Test the Proxy Connection

Click **Check Proxy** to ensure everything is working correctly.

### Save and Launch

Click **Create Profile** to save your settings, then click **Run** to launch your new profile and enjoy a secure browsing experience.

![](/src/assets/img/fingerprint/GoLogin/GoLogin3.avif)

**That's it!** You have successfully integrated LumiProxy proxies into GoLogin.
