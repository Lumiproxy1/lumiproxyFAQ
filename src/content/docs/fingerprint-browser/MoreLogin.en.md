# How to Configure LumiProxy with MoreLogin

> Enhance your multi-account management on MoreLogin with LumiProxy to achieve secure, anonymous browsing and better anti-detection protection.

## MoreLogin Proxy Integration

Here is how to easily integrate LumiProxy proxies with MoreLogin:

### Install MoreLogin

**Visit the [MoreLogin Official Website](https://www.morelogin.com/), download, and install the application.**


### Create an Account

Log in and begin the setup process.

### Create a New Profile

Click the **+ New profile** button to start filling out your profile information.
![](/src/assets/img/fingerprint/MoreLogin/MoreLogin1.avif)

### Set Up Initial Profile Details

Enter a name for your profile, select your preferred browser fingerprint options, and click **Advanced Create** to access the advanced settings.

![](/src/assets/img/fingerprint/MoreLogin/MoreLogin2.avif)
### Configure Proxy Settings

Scroll down to the **Proxies** section and input your LumiProxy connection details:

* **Proxy Type**: Select `HTTP` or `SOCKS5` (based on your proxy protocol).
* **Proxy Server**: Enter `us.lumiproxy.io`.
* **Proxy Port**: Use the port number provided in your [LumiProxy Dashboard](https://www.lumiproxy.com/user/dashboard/).
* **Proxy Account**: Enter your LumiProxy proxy `username`.
* **Proxy Password**: Enter your LumiProxy proxy `password`.

:::info ℹ️
**You can also specify a target country/region for your proxy session. For example, entering `lumi-username-area-US` will route your connection through a United States exit node.**
:::
![](/src/assets/img/fingerprint/MoreLogin/MoreLogin3.avif)

### Test the Proxy Connection

Click **Proxy Detection** or **Check Proxy** to ensure the proxy connects properly.

### Save and Launch

Click **Confirm** to save your configurations, then click **Start** to open your profile in a secure browsing environment.

![](/src/assets/img/fingerprint/MoreLogin/MoreLogin4.avif)

All done! You have successfully integrated LumiProxy proxies into MoreLogin.
