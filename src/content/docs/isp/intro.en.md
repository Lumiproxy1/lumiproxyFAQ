# Long-Term ISP Proxies · Introduction

**Long-Term ISP Proxies** are proxy products tailored for various business scenarios. This chapter will help you understand the product features, applicable scenarios, and basic architecture.
![](/src/assets/img/isp/3.png)

## Product Features

* Up to 12 hours of stable session time
* Coverage across major countries and regions worldwide
* Support for both HTTP and SOCKS5 protocols
* Username & Password authentication method

## Applicable Scenarios

* Cross-border e-commerce and social media account management
* Data collection and price monitoring
* Ad verification and SEO ranking tracking
