# Android

This chapter explains how to set up **Android**.

## Prerequisites

- A proxy account
- The client installed

## Configuration Steps - Wi-Fi


* Open **Settings** on your Android device.
* Tap **Wi-Fi**.
* Tap and hold your Wi-Fi network name, or tap the information icon next to it.
* Scroll to or tap **Advanced Settings**.
* Tap **Proxy** > **Manual**.
* Change the proxy settings. Enter the Hostname, Proxy Port, Username, and Password (e.g., `us.lumiproxy.com`). You can find the full list in your dashboard.
* Tap **Save** or exit the phone settings.
* To verify if the proxy is working properly, check your IP address at `api.ip.cc`.
