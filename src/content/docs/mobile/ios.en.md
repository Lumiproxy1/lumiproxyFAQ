# iOS

This chapter explains how to set up **iOS**.

## Prerequisites

- A proxy account
- The client installed

## Configuration Steps

### Settings - Wi-Fi
* 1. Open iPhone **Settings**.

* 2. Tap **Wi-Fi**.

* 3. Select the information icon (**i**) to the right of your connected network.

* 4. Scroll down to the **HTTP PROXY** section.

* 5. Tap **Manual**.

* 6. Edit your proxy settings (e.g., Server: `us.lumiproxy.com`, Port: `5888`). If your network IP is not whitelisted in the dashboard, enable **Authentication** and enter your **Username** and **Password**.

* 7. Tap back to the Wi-Fi selection window. This will save your proxy settings.

* 8. If you are using authentication, a second prompt may appear asking for your proxy username and password; if so, enter them again.








### Shadowrocke
* Click the **[+]** icon in the top right corner of the **Homepage**.
* **Type**: Select **HTTP** or **SOCKS5**.
* Enter the **IP**, **Port**, **Username**, and **Password**.
* Turn **TCP ON** and turn **UDP OFF**.
* Click **Save**.
